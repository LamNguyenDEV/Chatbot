# Events and Workshops

The Office of Global Engagement offers ongoing on-campus events, off-campus cultural excursions, and immigration workshops for international students and scholars.  If you have an idea for an event, workshop, program, or cultural excursion, let us know; we’re happy to try something new! For more information, please email [oge@montclair.edu](mailto:oge@montclair.edu)
